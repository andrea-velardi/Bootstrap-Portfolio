About Me

I was born in California but moved when I was very young to Philadelphia (Go Eagles!). When I turned 18, I knew I was ready to be "going going, back back, to Cali Cali" (R.I.P. Biggie Smalls). I moved away from the east coast to attend California Polytechnic State University in San Luis Obispo, CA. And if you are a big Operah fan, she quoted that city "the happiest city in America". And she is 100% right!! The city is located in a breathtaking crevice of the green rolling foothills, and backs up right to the most beautiful beaches you have ever laid eyes on. Cal Poly SLO, for short, is where I earned my B.S. in Business with a concentration in Human Resources and Management. I stayed in SLO to work full time at a software company, Mindbody Inc. for about a year in the HR Department, and then decided to move to Texas! I moved to Dallas to work for the Topgolf Corporate Office and absolutely love it. I have been with Topgolf for almost two years and it is a phenomenal organization. And the city of Dallas is a blast - the town culture is fun and engaging, with a plethera of great bars and restaurants. And that's my (short version) of my life story! Have fun exploring the page and more fun facts about moi.
